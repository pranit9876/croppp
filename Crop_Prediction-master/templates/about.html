Crop Prediction

A web application is developed, as an outcome. In the proposed web app application, the users should input their location to commence the prognostication process. Data pre processing is commenced when the location is received to the system.

The farmers will be gaining ample amount of profits from their yields as the price will be predicted along with the crops. No middle man will affect their economic gains as they can sell at reasonable prices. The old practice of cultivating hasty amount of crops and gaining not much of a profit will be eradicated. Farmers can sell their products directly through online markets straight to the customers. This factor will help to create revenue that is well predicted and expected. The ultimate result will be in incrementing agricultural contribution in Indian GDP and changing the mindset of youth about agriculture being the part and parcel of human well being.
